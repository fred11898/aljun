    • About

    At Pagadian Paws, our heartfelt mission is to create a world where every homeless pet finds a loving home. We're dedicated to rescuing, caring for, and advocating for the well-being of animals without homes. As you browse through our website, you'll find a wealth of resources, inspiring stories, and ways to get involved. Whether you're considering adopting a furry friend, eager to volunteer and make a meaningful impact, or seeking valuable information on responsible pet ownership, we're here to guide you.

    Mission and Vision

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to end pet euthanasia due to homelessness. We provide shelter, care, and promote responsible pet ownership to ensure every pet finds a loving home.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is a world where every homeless pet finds a loving home, and where pets are cherished as family, promoting respect and compassion for all animals. We believe in a collaborative effort to create a no-kill world, ensuring pet companionship for everyone.
